How Homza came to be.

  • 2014

    Who's up?

    Homza was born from a simple need: are my awesome room-mates home? Other questions as relevant followed: whn is the next subway? Will I need my umbrella? What's that moving dot in the sky? A UFO?

  • 2015

    A shared space has a life of its own

    Wether it's flast-sharing or a shared workspace, the people connected through that space form a community with its own problems, needs, and amazing moments. Homza makes sharing and enjoying the space easier.

  • 2016

    Share who you are, where you are

    Most of who you are is based on the media you consume. Today, who you are is shreded through the cloud on Netflix, Spotify, Youtube, LastFM, and others. Wether it is the space where you live or work, sharing who you are with who you live with will increase productivty and confort (or at least, that's what we believe in).
