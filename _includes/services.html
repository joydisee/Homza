Description

Homza can be used to perform several tasks among which:

Who's up?

Check who is physically present in a space through the network and get information about their taste, when they usually come in and out of the space, etc... Homza can be accessed either from a webpage with the RPi as a server or by plugging and turning ON a display to the RPi.

Weather/Transport/Sky/Sports Center

Homza will let you know if you need to grab an umbrella before leaving home, or if you favorite bus line is actually stopped for the next hour. Homza will also let you know about astronomical events happening in the sky, above your space, when they are happening, such as an ISS skycrossing. You'll also be able to check the latest world news and sports' results of your favourite teams using Homza.

Expand it !

Want to plug a Bluetooth thermometer to Homza? Piece of cake! You will need to write one simple class and you're done! The rest of the code will automatically store the data, then nicely displays it on the front, for everybody to enjoy !
